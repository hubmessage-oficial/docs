## Overview

Creates a new message template and submits it for Meta approval. The template starts with `PENDING` status and is updated to `APPROVED` or `REJECTED` after review.

Use the [Sync templates](/en/templates/sync-templates) endpoint to force a status update.

<Warning>
  The template name must be in `snake_case`, with no spaces or special characters. Once created, the name cannot be changed.
</Warning>

<Note>
  The `wabaId` is obtained from the [List WABAs](/en/templates/list-businesses) endpoint.
</Note>
